Consultancy

I can provide advice on using phased microphone arrays for wildlife tracking projects. I am especially interested in collaborating with ecology, biodiversity and animal behaviour projects, but I also work with commercial ecology consultancies, especially for bat activity surveys. Consultancy services include:

  1. Advice on planning an acoustic wildlife tracking project.

  2. Processing acoustic recordings. Flightpath provides software for processing recordings made by SkyTracer microphone arrays. We can also process the data for you.
    Contact flightpath for a quote.

  3. Bespoke array design for particular species or habitat sites. This includes a theoretical error analysis of the accuracy of the array for a particular situation, so the performance of the array can be assessed before production.

  4. Training on the effective use of microphone arrays.


    If you are planning a new project that needs acoustic wildlife tracking, please contact me.
